Peregrine Jet Uav

About Us

Goal Driven Florida Tech Undergrads

We are a team of three seniors in Computer Science working in conjunction with Aerospace and Mechanical engineering students, along with our faculty sponsors, with one goal in mind. We want to make a supersonic jet UAV capable of flight and autonomous capabilities. Together we will design, build, and program the UAV from the bottom up.
